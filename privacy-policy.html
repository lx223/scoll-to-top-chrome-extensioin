Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Scroll to Top is a browser extension that adds a simple scroll-to-top functionality to web pages. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles user data.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is accessed or stored
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No cookies or local storage is used for tracking
  • No analytics or telemetry is collected

Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to perform the scroll action on the tab you are currently viewing when you click the extension icon.
  • tabs: Required to re-inject the content script into existing tabs when the extension is installed or updated, ensuring the extension works immediately without requiring a page refresh.
  • scripting: Required to execute the scroll-to-top animation on the current page.
  • Host permissions (all URLs): Required to detect scroll position on web pages and enable the extension icon when you've scrolled down. This permission is necessary for the content script to communicate with the extension.

These permissions are used solely for the scroll-to-top functionality. No data from your browsing is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.
